\# Fintech – Affluent Customer Acquisition Model



\## Objective

Predict customers likely to maintain ₹40L+ TRB within 12 months.



\## Target Definition

target\_affluent = 1 if TRB >= 40,00,000

target\_affluent = 0 otherwise



\## TRB Formula

TRB = Savings + Fixed Deposits + Investments + Insurance AUM + Demat Holdings



\## KPIs

\- Affluent Conversion Rate

\- CAC

\- CPAA

\- ROAS

\- LTV



\## Revenue Assumptions

\- Revenue Yield: 2.5%

\- Retention: 5 years

\- Discount Rate: 10%

\- Cost per Lead: ₹3,000



\## Model Success Criteria

\- ROC-AUC > 0.75

\- Top 30% contains >60% affluent

\- 25% reduction in CPAA vs random targeting
